How it works

From idea to
published results

01

Initial Inquiry

Send a brief project summary to abc@uni-koeln.de. Include background, aims, and any preliminary data.

02

Consultation Meeting

We schedule a meeting with Prof. Christian Frezza and the team to discuss experimental strategy, sample requirements, and timelines.

03

Experiment & Analysis

Sample acquisition, QC-controlled runs, data processing, and biological interpretation — all performed collaboratively.

04

Publication Support

Data deposition (MetaboLights / Metabolomics Workbench), figure preparation, and co-authorship where appropriate.

Collaboration types

Who we work with

🏛️

Internal (UzK)

University of Cologne researchers benefit from priority access and subsidised rates. We actively seek projects where metabolomics can complement your existing experimental pipeline.

🔬

DFG & Funded Projects

We participate as co-investigators in DFG, BMBF, and EU-funded projects. Core facility costs can be included in grant budgets — contact us early in the application process.

🌍

External Academic

External academic institutions are welcome. We offer full-service collaborations including experimental design, acquisition, bioinformatics, and manuscript support.

⚗️

Method Development

Interested in developing new metabolomics workflows? We are open to long-term method development partnerships for novel sample types or metabolite classes.

Have a project in mind?

Request a meeting with Prof. Christian Frezza and the team. We respond to all inquiries within two business days.
